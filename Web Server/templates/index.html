What's Cool About Story Teller

Story Teller is an application that enables you to listen to books or stories every where in the world with extreme ease. Just take some pics using your phone, send it to our server, pour a cup of coffe, and enjoy listening to you story.

Our Application is designed to help you and everyone in the world to listen to books any where and while doing any thing such as: walking, driving, exercising or even enjoying a hot tea. We aim to help everyone whether he was an adult who is looking forward to enrich their knowledge, blind people who can't read or even a child who needs help before their sleep. Just install our application. and you're good to go
