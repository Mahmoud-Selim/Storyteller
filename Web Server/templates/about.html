About Story teller

Story Teller is an application that enables you to listen to books or stories anywhere in the world with extreme ease. Just take some pics using your phone, send it to our server, get your self a cup of coffe and enjoy listening to our product.

If you don't have time, not a book reader or your kid is learning how to read, We offer you a solution. We made an android application in which you can take pictures of your story or your book and let our application read it for you. We also have made it possible for you to save stories or books offline and listen to them later.

How it Works

First, you have to download our application. Then you should take pictures of your books or story, upload it to our server and get the text back. After that, you save it on your phone and enjoy listening to it offline.
